Professional Guide to Writing Party and Government Documents. Use when drafting, revising, or reviewing party and government documents (e.g., requests, notices, letters, summaries, minutes). Provides GB/T 9704-2012 formatting guidance, common document templates, language standards, and quality checks. Applicable for: (1) drafting new documents, (2) revising documents, (3) checking format compliance, (4) offering writing guidance, (5) reviewing document quality.
