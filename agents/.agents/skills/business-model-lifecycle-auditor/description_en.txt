Audit, design, and fix any business model across its full lifecycle. Produces a 15-component composite score, identifies the #1 constraint using Theory of Constraints, and delivers a prioritized action plan with experiment designs and scenario models. Covers canvas design (Lean/BMC/VPC), unit economics with industry benchmarks, retention and cohort analysis, pricing architecture with Van Westendorp WTP, competitive moat scoring, and TOC constraint identification. Supports SaaS, marketplace, e-commerce/DTC, agency, info products, and usage-based models. Triggers on: audit my business model, can this scale, help me price this, am I trading time for money, what breaks at 10x, stress test my model, check my unit economics, analyze churn/LTV/CAC, assess competitive moat, design pricing tiers, identify my biggest bottleneck. Do NOT trigger for pure financial projections, go-to-market strategy, or competitive analysis without business model context.
